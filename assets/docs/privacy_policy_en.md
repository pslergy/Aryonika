# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: November 19, 2024**

Welcome to Aryonika. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

## 1. Data We Collect
To provide accurate astrological calculations, we require:
*   **Birth Date and Time:** To calculate your natal chart.
*   **Place of Birth:** To determine time zone and house coordinates.
*   **Name:** For personalization.

We do NOT collect credit card information (processing is handled by payment gateways).

## 2. How We Use Data
*   **Calculations:** Your data is used solely for generating horoscopes, numerology reports, and compatibility analysis.
*   **Service Improvement:** Anonymous analytics help us improve the app.

## 3. Storage and Protection
Your data is stored on secure servers. We use modern encryption methods.

## 4. Data Deletion
You can delete your account and all associated data at any time via the app settings ("Profile" section).
